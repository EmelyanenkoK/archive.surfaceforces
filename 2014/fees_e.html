The Registration fees include:

For regular participants and PhD students: participation at the scientific sessions, conference issues, the welcome reception, on-site refreshments (“coffee breaks”), excursion and the Conference Dinner.
For the accompanying persons: the welcome reception, on-site refreshments (“coffee breaks”), excursion and the Conference Dinner.

The Accommodation fees include accommodation for 4 nights from May 12 till May 16, 2014, and all meals starting from dinner on May 12 and finishing with lunch on May 16.

The Accomodation fees depend on type of the room*:
• Standard Single - 270 Euro for 4 nights;
• Standard Double/Twin - 190 Euro per person (380 Euro per room) for 4 nights;

*Please note that there is a limited number of rooms of other types, like family 3-room apartment, luxury room with a fire-place etc. Please consult the website of the hotel, http://book.bestwestern.com/bestwestern/RU/Moscow-hotels/BEST-WESTERN-Country-Resort-Hotel/Hotel-Overview.do?propertyCode=91205
or in Russian at http://www.resort-country.ru/default.aspx?textpage=8
Those wishing other than a standard type of accommodation please apply to the Conference secretariat by E-mail surforc@phyche.ac.ru or write your requirements in the Comment box at the Registration page.
