Access to the Site

.

By Rail

A local train service links Zvenigorod to Moscow (Belorusski rail station). The trains depart with interval of hour and half, and the journey takes approximately 80 minutes. The tickets should be bought at the station. From Zvenigorod railway station, take the local bus No.48 to the “Pansionat Zvenigorodsky of Russian Academy of Sciences”.

.

By Bus

A regular bus No.452 service links Zvenigorod to Moscow (Metro station ”Kuntzevskaya”). The buses depart appproximately once an hour from 7.25 till 20.30, and the journey takes approximately one hour. The bus stop (not the terminal one!) is just opposite to the “Pansionat Zvenigorodsky of Russian Academy of Sciences”.

.

Connections at Sheremetyevo International Airport.

There is a regular bus service (approx. three times each hour) from Sheremetyevo airport to Moscow Metro Stations “Planernaya” (bus No.817) and “Rechnoy Vokzal” (bus No.851). The tickets can be bought in the bus, single fare is 10 roubles.
One trip by metro (any destination) costs 5 roubles.  

A taxi from Sheremetyevo Airport to Moscow city centre is about USD 60; to Zvenigorod – about USD 100.

.
